8 Smart Tricks to Pass JAMB

1️⃣ Master the JAMB Syllabus

Always read using the official Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board syllabus. JAMB only asks questions from topics listed there.

✅ Trick:
  • Print the syllabus
  • Tick topics you already understand

2️⃣ Practice Past Questions Daily

Most JAMB questions are repeated or slightly changed.

  • Solve at least 40–60 past questions daily
  • Focus on the last 20 years of past questions

3️⃣ Use the CBT Method While Studying

JAMB is a computer-based test (CBT).

  • Practice using CBT apps like:
    • MySchool JAMB CBT App
    • JAMB CBT Practice 2024
  • This helps you get used to the timer and interface ⏱️

4️⃣ Answer Easy Questions First

  • Answer easy questions first
  • Skip difficult ones and come back later
  • This helps you save time and secure marks

5️⃣ Use the Elimination Method

  • Cancel 2 wrong options
  • Choose between the remaining ones to increase your chance of success

6️⃣ Focus on Key Subjects

For many science students writing JAMB, important subjects include:

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • English

Spend more time on weak subjects.

7️⃣ Study Smart, Not Just Hard

  • Use the 2-hour study formula:
    • 45 minutes reading
    • 15 minutes break
    • 45 minutes practice questions
  • Your brain retains more this way 🧠

8️⃣ Read English Comprehension Carefully

  • Read the questions first
  • Then read the passage to find answers faster

✅ Bonus Secret

Many students fail because of time management, not lack of knowledge. If you practice speed + past questions, your score can reach 280+ in JAMB.
